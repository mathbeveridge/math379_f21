The recurrence relation is

\begin{equation*} \mbox{dist}(n,k) = \sum_{i=0}^{k-1} \mbox{dist}(n-k,i). \end{equation*}

Removing the first row of our partition results in a partition of \(n-k\) into at most \(k-1\) parts, and all parts distinct. The sum on the RHS counts the number of such partitions.
