For the given partition of 35, we have

\begin{equation*} \begin{array}{rcl} 35 &=& 7+5+5+3+3+3+3+3+1+1+1 \\ &=& 1 \cdot 7 + 2 \cdot 5 + (4+1) \cdot 3 + (2+1) \cdot 1 \\ &=& 7 + 10 + (12 + 3) + (2 + 1) \\ &=& 12 + 10 + 7 + 3 + 2 +1 \end{array} \end{equation*}

and these parts are all distinct!
