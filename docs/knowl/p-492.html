Consider the recurrence \(a_0=1\text{,}\) \(a_1=2\text{,}\) and \(a_n = na_{n-1} + n(n-1) a_{n-2}\) for \(n \geq 2\text{.}\)

  1. Find the exponential generating function \(A(x)\) for this sequence.

  2. Compare \(A(x)\) to \(F(x)\) from the previous problem. From this find a formula for \(a_n\) as a function of Fibonacci numbers \(f_k\text{.}\) In other words, you will get an expression for \(a_n\) that includes term(s) of the form \(f_k\text{.}\) (Note: the fact that \(f_0=0\) will be helpful as you change from functions back to power series.)
