Solution 1.
\begin{equation*} \begin{array}{rcl} |\mathrm{Aut}(K_n)| &=& n! \\ \ell(K_n)&=& 1 \\ |\mathrm{Aut}(K_n)| \cdot \ell(K_n)&=& n! \end{array} \end{equation*}

We explain why \(\ell(K_n)=1\text{.}\) All labelings of \(K_n\) are the same: every vertex is the same as every other vertex.
