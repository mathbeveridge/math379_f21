Exercise 2. \(k\)-sets and \((n-k)\)-sets.

For given positive integers \(0 \leq k \leq n\text{,}\)

  • Let \(X\) be the collection of \(k\)-subsets of \([n]\text{,}\) and

  • Let \(Y\) be the collection of \((n-k)\)-subsets.

(The \(k\)-subsets of \([n]\) are all the subsets of size \(k\)).
