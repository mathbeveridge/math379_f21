Exercise 3. Solving Another Recurrence.

Consider the recurrence relation \[ a_n = 4 a_{n-1} -1 \mbox{ for } n \geq 1 \] where \(a_0=3\)

  1. Let \(A(x)\) be the OGF for this series. Find a simple closed formula for \(A(x)\text{.}\)

  2. Use the Taylor series for the functions in part (a) to find a simple closed formula for \(a_n\text{.}\)
