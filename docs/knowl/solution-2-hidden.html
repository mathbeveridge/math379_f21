There are three distinct kinds of combo lunches: soup and salad, soup and sandwich, salad and sandwich. Note that you cannot get a Salad Sampler as part of this deal, so there are a total of 11 salad options. Also, a sandwich update is no longer possible. Therefore, there are a total of

\begin{equation*} 2 \times 11 + 2 \times 6 + 11 \times 6 = 22 + 12 + 66 = 100. \end{equation*}

possible lunches.
